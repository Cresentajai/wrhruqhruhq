INTELLICON

RULES

Intellicon is a technical event comprising two rounds focusing on metallurgy and material science fundamentals. In Word Wizard, teams of two provide clues to technical words for their partners to guess within 30 seconds. The top-scoring teams advance to Pick It!!, where they match materials to applications based on provided descriptions. The team with the most correct answers wins.

Duration

Time of the event and date


WOOTZ MAESTRO

Rules

Wootz Maestro is a quiz competition comprising two rounds. In Round 1, individuals participate in a preliminary quiz contest. Round 2 features the top two scorers from each department or college competing in a quiz master format, with the highest-scoring team declared the winner. No passes are allowed in this final round.

Duration

Time of the event and date

METALLOGRAPHY MANIA

Rules

Metallography Mania is a two-round competition where individuals analyze microstructures of given samples in Round 1, summarizing properties and applications. The top performers advance to Round 2, where they demonstrate metallography practices on provided specimens, with winners chosen based on work quality.

Duration

Time of the event and date

PAPER & POSTER PRESENTATION

Rules

Wootz presents an interdisciplinary paper and poster presentation event where students can showcase their work in Metallurgy/Material Science, Mechanical Engineering, Production Engineering, Civil Engineering, Industrial Engineering, and Automobile Engineering. Participants can register individually and submit their abstracts or posters via email. Presentations can be done individually or in teams of two. Selected participants will receive confirmation via email. Abstracts should be concise, not exceeding 250 words, and posters should be designed in A1 size using simple fonts. Details such as participant names, year of study, college name, contact information, and presentation title must be clearly mentioned in the abstract and email subject.

Duration

Time of the event and date
